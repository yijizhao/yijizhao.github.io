About me

I received the B.S. degree from Yunnan University, in 2016. I am currently a Ph.D. Candidate in School of Computer and Information Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University. And I am in institute of network science and intelligent systems (INSIS), supervised by Youfang Lin. My main research interests include data mining, spatial-temporal data forecasting, graph neural network. I focus on using AI to solve practical problems.
